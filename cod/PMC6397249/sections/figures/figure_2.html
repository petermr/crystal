X-ray diffractograms revealing the atomic structure of the primary-Au, MGMC, and metallic glass alloys. ( a) Au 65.2Cu 22.4Ag 12.4, ( b) Au 60Cu 23.5Ag 9.1Pd 1Si 6.4, ( c) Au 58Cu 24Ag 7.5Pd 1.5Si 9, ( d) Au 55.5Cu 24.4Ag 6.2Pd 2Si 11.9, and ( e) Au 50Cu 25.5Ag 3Pd 3Si 18.5. The diffractograms verify the amorphous structure of the metallic glass phase alloy Au 50Cu 25.5Ag 3Pd 3Si 18.5, the primary-Au structure of the particulate phase alloy Au 58Cu 24Ag 7.5Pd 1.5Si 9, and composite structure of the MGMC alloys Au 60Cu 23.5Ag 9.1Pd 1Si 6.4, Au 58Cu 24Ag 7.5Pd 1.5Si 9, and Au 65.2Cu 22.4Ag 12.4.
