Fabrication and characterization of Au@Ag SHINs as optical amplifiers. ( E) A series of SHINER spectra of DPA-TPM-G4 acquired with Au@Ag SHINs having 70-, 100-, and 115-nm particle sizes, compared with a normal Raman spectrum acquired without SHINs as a reference.
