S2 Fig

Comparison of MRI data and histological findings using embryonic rat samples (embryonic day 19). (A) Longitudinal sections of the left femur on embryonic day 19 with histological staining (hematoxylin and eosin staining and safranin O staining; ×20); 7-T MR images are shown. Red arrow: phase 4; yellow arrow: phase 5; asterisk: phase OS. (B) High magnification showing endochondral ossification. Histology belongs to phases 3–5 and phase OS. c: capillary; po: periosteal collar; os: osteoid. 3: phase 3; 4: phase 4; 5: phase 5. (C) 7-T MR images of the femur from human fetuses (CRL, 56.5 mm; ID: 52201), which have similar findings. Red arrow: phase 4; yellow arrow: phase 5. (TIF)
