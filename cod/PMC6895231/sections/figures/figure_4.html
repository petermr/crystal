2D-SAROM for a compacted tablet. a Chemical map obtained from the surface of a compacted tablet containing CBZD and PVP (2:1), b 2D-SAROM map showing the random orientation of CBZD particles on the surface of the tablet. The colors refer to the orientations as defined by the inverse pole figure (bottom right). SAROM was performed at a step size of 8 µm, map dimension 134 × 134 pixels or 1072 × 1072 µm, exposure time per step was 150 ms.
