Plate coupons of the primary-Au, MGMC, and metallic glass alloys. From left to right, plate coupons of the monolithic metallic glass Au 50Cu 25.5Ag 3Pd 3Si 18.5, MGMCs Au 55.5Cu 24.4Ag 6.2Pd 2Si 11.9, Au 58Cu 24Ag 7.5Pd 1.5Si 9 and Au 60Cu 23.5Ag 9.1Pd 1Si 6.4, and primary-Au alloy Au 65.2Cu 22.4Ag 12.4. The coupons reveal an alloy chromaticity that is compositionally-varying (from pale-gold to yellow-gold) yet visually unresolved to the naked eye.
